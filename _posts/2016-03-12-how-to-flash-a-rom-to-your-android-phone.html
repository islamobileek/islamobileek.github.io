Step Three: Download and Flash the ROM of Your Choice

flashing your ROM. First, you need to find the ROM you want.You can search in google or use theses links: this chart is a good place to start, as is list of the five most popular. Youca see XDA Developers forums to see what's available for your particular phone.

download the rom that you have found,using wifi and save it to your pc. It should come like ZIP file.You can download it from your computer and transfer it via USB or download it to the sd card.

To flash your ROM:

  1. Reboot your phone into Recovery mode.
  2. Head "Install" or "Install ZIP from SD Card" section
  3. select the ZIP file you downloaded
  4. Wait for the process to finish.
  5. you may also need to wipe your data and/or cache. In TWRP, you found it in "Wipe" section,in ClockworkMod,choose the "Wipe Data/Factory Reset" option or the "Wipe Cache Partition" option. When you're done, reboot into your new ROM.

wipe your data and cache?

  • If you flash a ROM different than the one you're currently running, you should wipe data and cache.This performs a factory reset on your phone, and will lose all your data.
  • if you want to flash a new version of a ROM you're already running, we recommend wiping your data and cache—but sufficient with wiping only the cache.

When you reboot, you should be in your new ROM, ready to use it! But what? There's no Play Store?
